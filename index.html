Chris Ratterree

Independent design engineer with a focus on automotive and insurance industry projects.

I started my design career analog style with a sword brush and a can of One Shot at around 15 years old. I learned layout and design with masking tape and a yard stick while lettering and painting race cars, helmets and signs.
In the mid 2000's I started a side business doing vinyl signs. I mainly did cut vinyl signs and race car graphics. I also learned Photo Shop & Illustrator while preparing files for sublet printing jobs.
Fast forward to 2021 and I am back in front of a computer learning more about code. I have some of my projects below and you can check out my resume here. Let me know if you have a project i can help with.
